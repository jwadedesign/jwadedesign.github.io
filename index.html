Illumination

A successful lighting scheme should compliment an interior or exterior; Its presence should be a theatrical experience the user can enjoy. The interest and comfort of the environment depends greatly on its texture, shadow, highlight, glow, darkness and accent; all requirements on every surface of the well-designed space.

At J Wade lighting design is considered as a seamless layering and it is by the intelligent grouping of lights into these layers that allows the manipulation of a space to suit the desired task or ambience. We consider our lighting plan to be one of the most important aspects of your project. We begin working on the lighting plan early in the design process, incorporating each layer into the overall scheme, not as an afterthought.
